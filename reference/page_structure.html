Services We Provide

Our Book Publishing Services And Expertise

At Book Publishing Partner, we help aspiring authors self-publish effortlessly. With professional editing, formatting, cover design, and global distribution, we make your publishing journey simple and successful.

Ghostwriting

Have an idea for a potential bestseller? Share it with us! Our talented team of professional ghostwriters will help turn your concept into a fully-fledged book. Whether you have a rough idea or a detailed plot, we’ll take care of everything – from research and story expansion to outlining, drafting chapters, revising, and editing. Once your manuscript is polished, we’ll finalize it for publication. We specialize in various genres, including self-help books, and our native writers are experts in crafting high-quality drafts. Don't keep your great idea to yourself – hire a skilled ghostwriter today and bring your book to life!

Editing

Congratulations on writing your first draft! Now, are you ready to transform it into a polished, error-free masterpiece? Invest in our professional editing services to perfect your manuscript for publication. Simply email us your draft, and our expert editors will conduct an in-depth alpha read, offer valuable feedback, and refine your manuscript with your input in mind. We specialize in copy editing, line editing, developmental editing, and thorough proofreading. Let our experienced editors help you elevate your book to the next level. Hire a professional editor today and get started on perfecting your manuscript for publication!

Publishing

Congratulations on completing your writing, editing, and formatting! You're now just one step away from joining the ranks of self-published authors. Send us your formatted manuscript, and we'll help you publish across top platforms like KDP, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, Smashwords, Apple iBook Store, IngramSpark, and Google Books. Our team will assist you in selecting a compelling title, designing a unique book cover, formatting your manuscript to meet publishing standards, crafting a professional book description, and creating a powerful book launch strategy. Hire a professional publisher today, share your manuscript, and get your book published with ease!

Marketing

Are you a published author? Fantastic! Share your book details with us, and our expert marketing team will create a tailored strategy to boost your book's visibility and maximize your royalties. With the right plan, organization, and consistent effort, we’ll help propel your book from the bottom of the shelf to the top. From effective book marketing techniques and compelling back cover copy to optimized listings and marketing materials, we’ve got everything you need. Hire a professional marketing consultant today, share your published book, and start increasing your book sales now!
